## Raw Question

How severe are the unmitigated losses once a 7DTE iron condor reaches the common
stress trigger of short-strike breach or short-side delta `>= 0.25`?

## Notes

- Actor: A single-position 7DTE SPXW iron condor strategy using the shared baseline entry and expiry-selection rules.
- Constraint: No repair action is allowed after trigger detection; the strategy only logs the event.
- Mechanism: Hold the original condor unchanged after trigger so post-trigger path dependency is fully exposed.
- Persistence: The baseline should show whether tail behavior is concentrated in a small number of triggered trades across the full 2022 to 2025 window.
- Kill condition: If trigger events are rare or post-trigger damage is modest, the repair project has less leverage than expected.

## Primary Prediction

Baseline behavior should show the largest post-trigger downside and the highest
frequency of full-width losses among the five 7DTE variants.

## Secondary Predictions

- Trigger logs will show that the worst outcomes cluster around fast directional moves into one tested side.
- The baseline run should provide the widest dispersion of post-trigger outcomes for comparison against repair variants.

Run `qc/spxw_7dte_baseline` and use its trigger log plus tail metrics as the
