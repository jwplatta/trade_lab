## Raw Question

Does recentering in the middle of the trade lifecycle work better than
recentering earlier at 6 to 7 DTE?

## Notes

- Actor: A single-position 7DTE SPXW iron condor strategy using the baseline `IronCondorFinder`.
- Constraint: Keep the baseline entry timing, pricing, and expiry-selection rules unchanged.
- Mechanism: If short-leg delta reaches `0.20` while the trade is in the `3-5` DTE bucket, close and reopen a fresh condor on the same expiry.
- Persistence: Any benefit should survive across multiple volatility regimes.
- Kill condition: The recenter adds churn without improving drawdown or loss concentration versus baseline and exp01.

## Primary Prediction

Mid-DTE recentering may have a stronger impact on tail control than high-DTE
recentering because the trade is closer to the loss-acceleration phase.

## Secondary Predictions

- Mid-DTE recentering will likely cost more in realized losses and fees than the 6 to 7 DTE version.
