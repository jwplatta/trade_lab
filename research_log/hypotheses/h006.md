## Raw Question

Is an early late-cycle time extension still useful once the position reaches 1 to 2 DTE?

## Notes

- Actor: A single-position 7DTE SPXW iron condor strategy using the baseline `IronCondorFinder`.
- Constraint: Trigger only before `12:00` ET while the trade is at `1-2` DTE.
- Mechanism: If short-leg delta reaches `0.25` or spot breaches a short strike, close and reopen on the next valid expiry.
- Persistence: The rule should improve the late-cycle left tail beyond isolated event days.
- Kill condition: The roll just extends risk without improving drawdown or worst-loss concentration.

## Primary Prediction

Early late-cycle roll-forward should help more than doing nothing, but less than earlier repairs.

## Secondary Predictions

- The rule may generate a large order count because late-cycle breaches cluster during volatile periods.
